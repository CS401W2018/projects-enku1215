Structural Tags

Structural Tags (HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER).
What goes in the header?- The title or it can be pictures represetning your page purpose. Some pages has search bar and logos.
What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website? - Links to the more information of your page go in the Nav. It can be eiter in the header or outside the header.
The Nav tag includes our own webpage links only.
What goes in Main? - Our main message or main contents should go in the main tag.
What goes in the Footer? - It includes copyright and privacy notice and other legal stuff. It can also includes more links about more information about your page.
HTML Validation - It checks our webpage code errors. It validates our html.
