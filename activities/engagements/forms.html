The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do? - These two attributes are used to send datas to the server. The action attribute specifies where the data should be sent for processing.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do? - Method attribute specifies how to send the data to the server.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"? - We use "get" when we are reading information, "post" is for when you are adding information or changing your informaion.
