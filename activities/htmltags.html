Phrase Elements

A phrase is a small group of words that communicates a concept but is not a full sentence. You use phrases in your writing & your speech every day. There are lots of different kinds of phrases, some of which play a technical role in your writing and others that play a more illustrative role. No matter which role a phrase is playing, it is achieving one simple goal: making your sentences richer by giving your words context, detail, and clarity.
