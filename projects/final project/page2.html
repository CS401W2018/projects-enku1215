The problem

Destination Map

Because Mongolia is huge, it is as twice as the size of Texas. Tourists have to travel at least 5-9 hours to reach a single destination with only one or two limited activities available. In the country, the roads are not always good. Some the places still have the dust road.

Our solution

One Location

We're bringing all of them into one location. Our guests should be able to experience most of the culture, art, and lifestyle in one place. The center will be located only 1.5 hrs away from the city. We will host mini festivals everyday that travelers only able to watch once a year.
