The writer of "Spy Family": Tatsuya Endo

Main Page   Manga Artist
Tatsuya Endo is famour manga artist in Japan. He was born in July 23, 1980. Since he was a little kid, he would read mangas and dream about becoming a manga artist. In other words, he is doing his dream job. At the beginning of his career, he used to write mangas that have dark tones like murderers or badside people. His partner has encouraged him to write brighter mangas.

He started "Spy x Family" in 2019 and debuted in 2022. It has became a masterpiece now. Millions of copies have sold online. His characters illustrates how people's mind shift fast between positive and negative .
He has submitted his several works on Shonen Jump.

Some of his works:

"Seibu Yugi" and "Blade of the Moon Princess" in 2000.
"Witch Craze"and "PMG-0" from 2001-2004.
"Tista" /serial/ in 2004.
"I Spy" in 2018.
"Spy x Family" /serial/ in 2019.
