Explanation

These Chinese words can be used to express when talk about your exams with your classmate. For example, when you ask your classmate "how did he/she do on the exam?", you can use the following new words. The difference between "fuxi" and "yuxi" is one is used when you review after you learn something. The other one is used when you study something before it has been taught.
